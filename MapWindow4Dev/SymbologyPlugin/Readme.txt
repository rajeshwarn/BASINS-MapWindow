------------------------------------------------------
             MapWindow Symbology plug-in
------------------------------------------------------

Application plug-in meant to provide functionality 
for visualization of vector layers (shapefiles)
from color schemes to labels and charts. 
Is in development phase now. Is scheduled for release
by the end of summer 2010.              

To test the plug-in place mwSymbology.dll in 
Bin\ApplicationPlugins folder. Rightclick on the
shapefile layer in the legend control. Choose 'properties'
item holding Shift button pressed.
